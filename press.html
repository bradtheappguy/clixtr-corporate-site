CLIXTR Is The Ultimate Social Mobile App Company

Press

Who are we? We're Clixtr (not Clixter, Clickstr, Clixstr, or even Clixster) and we turn your smartphone into a smartcamera! Since October 2008 we've been working hard to surprise and delight our users, snapping photos, and sharing events along the way. We received seed funding from DFJ in Menlo Park in March 2009, and we launched our first product at TechCrunch50 2009.

We provide a (patent-pending) platform for our users to create group photo-sharing albums which are geo-tagged. Clixtr gives users the ability to share their photos via the Clixtr mobile app in real time with friends and family as well as allow users to watch events unfold from around the corner and around the world. Additionally, users can upload content through the Clixtr website or watch event photo streams.

In December 2009, we received an Emerging Technology award for Social Networking from the Silicon Valley Business Journal. In January 2010, we won the Mobile Apps Showdown at the Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas.

If you'd like more information on Clixtr, visit our blog or email us at info@clixtr.com.
